01 — Vision & Objective

The Home of
Everything Outdoor.

30
minutes a day — avg. urban Indian spends outdoors

We are not building a product. We are building a cultural movement — making the outdoors a habit, a status, and an identity for Bangalore's urban population.

From a morning walk in Cubbon Park to a weekend at a working farm, OTO is where the shift begins.

Vision

When people in Bangalore think outdoor — they think OTO. We become the default platform, the trusted community, and the cultural anchor for all outdoor life in the city.

Objective

Build the culture first. Prove the movement through real events, real community, and real hours logged outdoors. Then scale it with technology and a national campaign.

02 — The Name

Still Hunting
For The Right Name.

The name is one of the most important decisions we make. We are not rushing it. Four options have been evaluated so far — some ruled out for clear reasons, one front-runner emerging — but we are keeping this open until we are fully confident.

Options Evaluated So Far
✕ Ruled Out
1000 Hours Outside
Already an established movement in the US with a large Instagram following. No point competing when we can own something uniquely ours.
✕ Ruled Out
All Things Outdoor
Reads like a listing platform or a marketplace. Passive. Doesn't carry the assertive, movement energy we need.
✕ Ruled Out
Own Your Outdoors
Acronym problem — OYO is taken and carries entirely different brand baggage. Cannot own that name.
○ Front-Runner — Under Review
Own The Outdoor — OTO
Active, commanding, ownable. "Own" creates agency. "The Outdoor" frames it as a territory, not a category. OTO is clean and distinctive — but we want to be sure before we commit.
How We Will Finalise The Name
1
Marketing Team Review
Present shortlisted names to the internal marketing team for strategic and brand fit evaluation
2
Office Name Contest
Open it up internally — invite the team to submit and vote on names. Good names can come from anywhere
3
External Naming Inputs
Additional name suggestions will be shared by Vachana for consideration alongside internal options
What the Name Must Feel Like

Bold. Not wellness. Not corporate. Not preachy. Nike-level confidence. The outdoors isn't a retreat — it's a claim. Whatever we name it, it must own that feeling.

04 — The Farm Guide

The Experience
Needs an Expert.

Event production, logistics, promotion and vendor management — the existing team handles all of that. What they cannot provide is what makes OTO different from any other event: a deep, genuine connection with the outdoors. That requires a dedicated hire.

Phase 1 — Dedicated Hire
Farm Guide ×1

This person is not a coordinator or a manager. They are an outdoor expert — someone who lives and breathes nature, and whose entire job is to make every visitor’s time on the farm feel alive, meaningful and unforgettable.

What They Do
Lead Treks & Nature Walks
Plan and guide outdoor trails, sunrise walks, forest routes and exploration sessions for all age groups
Engage Visitors & Children
Make every visitor — especially children — feel curious, active and connected to the farm and nature around them
Touch With Nature Activities
Conduct hands-on sessions — soil science, plant identification, composting, birdwatching, seed sowing and farm ecology
Guide Day & Two-Day Visits
Own the full guest experience arc — arrival orientation, activity flow, meal context, evening session and departure
Outdoor Activity Expert
Can lead yoga, basic fitness sessions, team games and other outdoor physical activities as needed
Who They Are — Ideal Profile
Nature Expert First.
Communicator Second.
Background in ecology, environmental science, botany, biology or outdoor education
Knowledgeable in local flora, fauna, birds, insects, soil and farm science
Comfortable leading groups of all ages — families, corporates, children
Warm, engaging communicator who can make science feel approachable and fun
Physically active and comfortable in all outdoor and farm conditions
Passionate about outdoor life — this is not just a job for them
What They Do NOT Do
✕ Event production or logistics
✕ Vendor or partner management
✕ Promotion or marketing
✕ Ticket sales or admin
The existing events team owns all of the above. This role is purely about the guest experience on the ground.
🌿

The Farm Guide is what turns a day on the farm from a nice outing into something people talk about for weeks. This role is the heart of OTO.
